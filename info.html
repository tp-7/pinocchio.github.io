About Pinocchio

Pinocchio is an innovative art showcase that celebrates the creativity of the Counterparty and Dogeparty communities. Inspired by the vibrant and eclectic Pepe art movement, Pinocchio brings together a collection of unique and captivating artwork.

Our talented artists use various mediums and techniques to express their artistic visions. Each artwork tells a story and invites viewers to explore the depths of imagination.

Pinocchio serves as a platform to showcase the extraordinary talent within the Counterparty and Dogeparty communities. It is a testament to the power of art to create connections, foster inspiration, and bring people together.
