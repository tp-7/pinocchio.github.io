Donna Gray Boyd

Donna Gray Boyd, a dedicated mental health professional with a profound commitment to uplifting others, spearheaded the creation of these initiatives. Designed to bring joy to VA patients, their families, and the compassionate medical teams serving them, these projects also aim to spotlight the invaluable contributions of VA Voluntary Service. By fostering awareness and encouraging new volunteers to join the cause, Donna's endeavors embody her unwavering dedication to making a meaningful difference in the lives of others.
